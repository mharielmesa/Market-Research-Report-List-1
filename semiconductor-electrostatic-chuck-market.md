<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor electrostatic chuck market can be categorized into two types: Coulomb Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck. </p><p>Coulomb Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck utilizes the concept of Coulomb's law to generate electrostatic forces that hold semiconductor wafers during processing. These chucks have the advantage of high holding force but are limited in terms of wafer size.</p><p>On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Semiconductor Electrostatic Chuck utilizes the phenomenon of the elastomeric material's diaphragm to generate uniform holding force across the entire wafer surface. These chucks are capable of accommodating larger wafer sizes but have reduced holding force compared to Coulomb Type chucks.</p></p>
