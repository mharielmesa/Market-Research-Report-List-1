<p><p>The Industrial Current Sensor market can be divided into two types: Closed-loop Sensors and Open-loop Sensors. Closed-loop Sensors are designed to measure current by using a feedback loop that continuously adjusts the output to match the input. These sensors provide accurate measurements and are commonly used in applications where precision is crucial. On the other hand, Open-loop Sensors do not rely on a feedback loop and are more economical. They are suitable for applications where absolute accuracy is not the main concern.</p></p>
