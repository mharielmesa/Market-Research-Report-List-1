<p><p>The nuclear power pump market consists of different types of pumps, including cartridge pumps, vertical pumps, and horizontal pumps. Cartridge pumps are compact and easy to install, making them suitable for various applications in nuclear power plants. Vertical pumps are designed with a vertical shaft and are ideal for handling high flow rates and low-pressure applications. Horizontal pumps, on the other hand, have a horizontal shaft and are widely used for medium to high-pressure operations. These different types cater to different requirements within the nuclear power industry.</p></p>
