<p><p>The wafer processing and assembly equipment market consists of various types of equipment used in the manufacturing of semiconductors. Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) equipment is used to remove excess material from wafers. Etching equipment is utilized to selectively remove layers from the wafer's surface. Thin Film Deposition equipment applies thin layers of material onto the wafer. Photoresist Processing equipment is employed for masking and patterning the wafer. Assembly equipment is responsible for packaging and assembling multiple semiconductor devices onto a single chip.</p></p>
