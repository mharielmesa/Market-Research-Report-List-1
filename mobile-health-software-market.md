<p><p>The mobile health software market is categorized into two types - on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises software is installed and operated on local servers within healthcare organizations, offering them complete control over data storage and security. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing healthcare providers to access data from anywhere. Cloud-based solutions offer scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, while on-premises solutions provide greater control and customization options. Both options cater to different needs and preferences within the mobile health industry.</p></p>
