<p><p>Audio DACs stand for Digital-to-Analog Converters used in audio devices to convert digital audio signals into analog signals for playback through speakers or headphones. There are two main types: Audio A/D Converters, also known as Analog-to-Digital Converters, which convert analog audio signals into digital signals for recording or processing, and Audio D/A Converters, which convert digital audio signals back into analog signals for audio playback. These converters are crucial in the audio industry as they ensure high-quality sound reproduction and are used in various applications like music production, home theater systems, and portable audio devices.</p></p>
