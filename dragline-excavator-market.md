<p><p>The dragline excavator market is segmented into two main types: diesel-mechanical and diesel-electric hybrid. In the diesel-mechanical segment, the excavator's power is derived solely from a diesel engine, which drives all the mechanical components. On the other hand, a diesel-electric hybrid excavator combines a diesel engine with an electric generator, which powers electric motors that drive the mechanical components. This hybrid system aims to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions by utilizing the benefits of both diesel and electric power sources in the excavator's operation.</p></p>
