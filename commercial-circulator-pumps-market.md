<p><strong>The Commercial Circulator Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Circulator Pump</li><li>Horizontal Circulator Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial circulator pumps market consists of two main types: vertical circulator pumps and horizontal circulator pumps. Vertical circulator pumps are designed to be installed vertically, with the motor on top and the impeller at the bottom. They are typically used in buildings with limited floor space. On the other hand, horizontal circulator pumps are installed horizontally, with the motor and impeller aligned on the same axis. They are commonly used in buildings with more available floor space. Both types of pumps are used to move large volumes of liquid in commercial applications.</p></p>
