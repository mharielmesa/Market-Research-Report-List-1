<p><strong>The Hotel Revenue Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>On-Premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hotel Revenue Management System market can be divided into two types: Cloud Based and On-Premises. Cloud Based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing hotels to store and access data from anywhere. These systems are typically scalable, cost-effective, and require minimal maintenance. On the other hand, On-Premises systems are installed locally within the hotel's servers, requiring hardware and software maintenance, but offering greater control and data security. Both types have their advantages and choosing the right one depends on the hotel's specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
