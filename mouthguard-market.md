<p><strong>The Mouthguard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preformed Mouthguard</li><li>Thermoformed Mouthguard</li><li>Custom Mouthguard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are three main types of mouthguards in the market: preformed, thermoformed, and custom mouthguards. Preformed mouthguards are mass-produced and come in standard sizes, providing a basic level of protection. Thermoformed mouthguards are made by heating and shaping a moldable material to fit an individual's teeth. They offer a better fit and higher level of protection than preformed mouthguards. Custom mouthguards are specifically tailored to an individual's teeth based on dental impressions. They provide the best fit, comfort, and protection, but are more expensive than the other types.</p></p>
