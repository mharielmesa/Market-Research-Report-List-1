<p><p>The food plastic packaging market consists of three main types of materials: thermoset polymers, thermoplastic polymers, and elastomers. Thermoset polymers are strong and rigid plastics that are typically molded into a specific shape and cannot be reshaped once set. Thermoplastic polymers are versatile plastics that can be melted and reshaped multiple times. Elastomers are flexible and stretchable plastics that can easily return to their original shape after being stretched. These different types of plastics offer various benefits and are used in food packaging to cater to different packaging needs.</p></p>
