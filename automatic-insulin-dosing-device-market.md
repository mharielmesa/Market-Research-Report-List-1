<p><strong>The Automatic Insulin Dosing Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threshold Suspended Device Systems</li><li>Hybrid Closed-loop APDS</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threshold Suspended Device Systems are insulin dosing devices that automatically suspend insulin delivery when glucose levels reach a preset threshold to prevent hypoglycemia. Hybrid Closed-loop APDS, also known as Artificial Pancreas Device Systems, combine continuous glucose monitoring with an insulin pump to automatically adjust insulin delivery based on real-time glucose readings. Other markets in the automatic insulin dosing device industry may include sensor-augmented pump therapy and predictive low glucose suspend systems, which also aim to improve blood sugar control and reduce the risk of hypoglycemia in individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
