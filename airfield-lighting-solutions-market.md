<p><p>The Airfield Lighting Solutions market includes various types of lighting systems used in airports and airfields. Runway lights are used to guide aircraft during takeoff and landing, ensuring safe operations. Taxiway lights help pilots navigate on the ground, guiding them to and from the runway. Ground guidance lights assist in guiding aircraft to parking positions and various airport facilities. Obstruction lights warn pilots about tall structures like towers or buildings near the landing area.
