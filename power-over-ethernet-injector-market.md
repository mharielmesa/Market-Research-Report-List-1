<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) Injector market consists of two major types: single-port Midspan and multi-port Midspan. A single-port Midspan refers to a PoE device that provides power to a single PoE-enabled device, such as an IP camera or wireless access point. On the other hand, a multi-port Midspan can power multiple PoE-enabled devices simultaneously, typically ranging from 4 to 48 ports. These PoE injectors are widely used in networking installations where power and data transmission need to be combined into a single Ethernet cable, simplifying installation and reducing costs.</p></p>
