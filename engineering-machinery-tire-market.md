<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineering machinery tires are classified into two main types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of fabric cords crisscrossing at a 30-45 degree angle, providing strong sidewalls and resistance to punctures. Radial tires have layers of steel belts running across the tire at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, offering higher flexibility and fuel efficiency. Both tire types cater to the specific needs of different engineering machinery, providing optimal performance and durability in various working conditions.</p></p>
