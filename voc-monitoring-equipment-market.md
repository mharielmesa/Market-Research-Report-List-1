<p><p>VOC Monitoring Equipment refers to devices used for monitoring Volatile Organic Compounds in the air. There are two main types: PID (Photo-Ionization Detector) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor market. PIDs work on the principle of ionizing VOCs, while MOS market uses a semiconductor sensor. PIDs are known for their high sensitivity and can detect low levels of VOCs, making them suitable for industrial applications. On the other hand, MOS market devices offer reliable and cost-effective monitoring, making them popular for indoor air quality assessments. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring environmental safety and health.</p></p>
