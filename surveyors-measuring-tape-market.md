<p><p>Surveyors measuring tape market is divided into three types based on the type of end they have - Hook End, Ring End, and Zero End. Hook End tapes are equipped with a metal hook that can be easily hooked onto objects for accurate measurements. Ring End tapes have a round ring at the end, useful for measuring circular objects like trees or poles. Zero End tapes start the measurement from the end of the tape, enabling precise measurements of the starting point. These variations cater to different surveying needs and provide diverse measuring options for professionals.</p></p>
