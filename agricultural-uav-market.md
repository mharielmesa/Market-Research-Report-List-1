<p><ul><li>Fixed-wing Agricultural UAV</li><li>Multi-rotor Agricultural UAV</li><li>Hybrid Agricultural UAV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The agricultural UAV market includes three primary types of drones, each with different capabilities. Fixed-wing agricultural UAVs are similar to airplanes and are best suited for large farms as they cover more area efficiently, collecting data and spraying pesticides or fertilizers. Multi-rotor agricultural UAVs, such as quadcopters, are more versatile and capable of hovering, making them ideal for small or irregularly shaped fields. Hybrid agricultural UAVs combine the advantages of both fixed-wing and multi-rotor drones, offering a balance between efficiency and flexibility. They are suitable for medium-sized farms requiring both speed and versatility.</p></p>
