<p><p>Neutron detection equipment refers to devices used to detect and measure the presence of neutrons. There are several types of detectors available in the market. </p><p>Lithium large-area neutron detectors are designed to have a large detection area, allowing for the detection of a higher number of neutrons.</p><p>Fast neutron detectors are capable of quickly detecting high-energy neutrons and are often used in applications such as nuclear power plants.</p><p>Scintillation neutron detectors use scintillating materials that emit light when struck by neutrons, providing a way to detect their presence.</p><p>Semiconductor neutron detectors utilize semiconductor materials to detect neutrons and convert them into electrical signals for measurement. They offer advantages such as high sensitivity and fast response time.</p></p>
