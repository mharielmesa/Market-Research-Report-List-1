<p><p>The fixed fire hydrant market can be classified into two types: indoor and outdoor. Indoor fire hydrants are typically installed indoors, such as in large buildings or industrial facilities, to provide a readily accessible water supply for firefighting purposes. These hydrants are usually connected to a building's internal water distribution system. On the other hand, outdoor fire hydrants are installed along streets, roads, or open spaces and are connected to the external water supply network. They serve as crucial firefighting equipment for public areas and help ensure quick response to fires in the vicinity.</p></p>
