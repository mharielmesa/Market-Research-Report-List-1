<p><p>The 3D printing of metals market can be divided into different types, including Selective Laser Melting (SLM), Electronic Beam Melting (EBM), and others. Selective Laser Melting involves using a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder layers together, while Electronic Beam Melting utilizes an electron beam to melt metal powder layer by layer. These two methods are widely used in the industry. Apart from SLM and EBM, there are other metal 3D printing techniques, such as Binder Jetting, Direct Energy Deposition, and Laminated Object Manufacturing, each with its unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
