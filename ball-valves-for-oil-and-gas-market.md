<p><p>Ball valves are commonly used in the oil and gas industry due to their reliability and efficiency. There are three main types of ball valves for this market: flanged, threaded, and socket-weld ball valves. Flanged ball valves have flanges on both ends, enabling them to be easily connected to pipelines. Threaded ball valves have threads on both ends, allowing for easy installation and removal. Socket-weld ball valves have sockets to connect to pipelines through welding. Each type offers different advantages and is chosen based on specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
