<p><p>Power inductors are essential components in 5G technology. They come in different market types, including Through-Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD). Through-Hole power inductors are designed for mounting on printed circuit boards (PCBs) using leaded technology, providing secure connections but requiring manual assembly. On the other hand, SMD power inductors are smaller in size and can be mounted directly on the surface of PCBs using automated assembly machines, ensuring precise and efficient placement. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the smooth operation and power management of 5G networks.</p></p>
