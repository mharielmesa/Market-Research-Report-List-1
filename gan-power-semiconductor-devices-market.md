<p><p>GaN (Gallium Nitride) Power Semiconductor Devices are a type of semiconductor devices that utilize GaN material to provide high-speed, high power, and low resistance capabilities. The increasing demand for smaller, lighter, and more efficient power electronic devices in industries such as automotive, aerospace, consumer electronics, and telecommunications is driving the market growth. GaN power devices are widely used in applications such as power supplies, inverters, UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems, and electric vehicles, which further contributes to market expansion.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising adoption of GaN-based RF power devices in the telecommunications sector for high-speed data transmission and wireless charging applications is also fueling the market growth.
