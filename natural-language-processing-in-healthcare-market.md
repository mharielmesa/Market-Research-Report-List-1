<p><p>The Natural Language Processing (NLP) market in healthcare comprises two types: Hybrid NLP and Statistical NLP. Hybrid NLP combines rule-based and statistical techniques to analyze and process medical data. It leverages predefined rules and statistical algorithms to extract meaningful information. On the other hand, Statistical NLP focuses on using statistical algorithms to interpret natural language, allowing healthcare providers to analyze patient data and generate insights. Both types of NLP play crucial roles in automating medical processes, improving patient care, and enabling efficient clinical decision-making.</p></p>
