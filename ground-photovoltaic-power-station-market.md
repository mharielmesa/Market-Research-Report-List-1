<p><p>The Ground Photovoltaic Power Station market consists of two main types: Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaic Power Station and Thin Film Photovoltaic Power Station. Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaic Power Station utilizes solar cells made from crystalline silicon, which are more efficient and have a longer lifespan. On the other hand, Thin Film Photovoltaic Power Station uses solar cells made from various thin films, such as amorphous silicon or cadmium telluride. Thin film technology is less expensive and more flexible, allowing for easier integration into different surfaces. Both types contribute to the growth of the photovoltaic power industry.</p></p>
