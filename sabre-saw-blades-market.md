<p><strong>The Sabre Saw Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>T-Shank</li><li>U-Shank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sabre saw blades are categorized into two main types: T-Shank and U-Shank. T-Shank blades feature a straight, T-shaped shank that fits securely into the saw, making it easy to insert and remove the blade quickly. These blades are commonly used in most modern sabre saws. On the other hand, U-Shank blades have a U-shaped shank with a locking mechanism that allows for easy blade changes. While T-Shank blades are more popular and widely available, U-Shank blades are still used in some older models of sabre saws. Both types of blades serve the same purpose of cutting through various materials.</p></p>
