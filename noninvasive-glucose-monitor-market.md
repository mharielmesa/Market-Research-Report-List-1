<p><ul><li>Wearable Monitor</li><li>Non-wearable Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The noninvasive glucose monitor market consists of two main types: wearable monitors and non-wearable monitors. Wearable monitors are devices that can be worn on the body, such as smartwatches or adhesive patches, and continuously measure glucose levels throughout the day. Non-wearable monitors, on the other hand, are usually stationary devices that use different techniques like optical sensors or spectroscopy to measure glucose levels without the need for puncturing the skin. Both types aim to provide a convenient and painless alternative to traditional blood glucose monitoring methods for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
