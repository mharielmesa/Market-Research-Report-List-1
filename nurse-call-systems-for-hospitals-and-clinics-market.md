<p><p>Nurse Call Systems are communication devices used in hospitals and clinics to enable patients to request assistance from nurses or healthcare providers. There are two types of Nurse Call Systems: wired and wireless. Wired systems consist of physical cables that connect patient rooms to a central nurse station, allowing for immediate communication. Wireless systems, on the other hand, utilize wireless technology such as Wi-Fi or radio-frequency signals to transmit patient requests, providing flexibility and mobility. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring prompt and efficient healthcare services.</p></p>
