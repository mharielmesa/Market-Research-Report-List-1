<p><p>The wet shaver market consists of different types of shavers, namely manual shavers, rotary shavers, and reciprocating shavers. Manual shavers require the users to manually glide the blade over their skin. Rotary shavers have circular blades that move in a circular motion, allowing for versatile shaving angles. Reciprocating shavers have back-and-forth moving blades, providing a close and precise shave. These three market segments cater to different shaving preferences, offering options to consumers based on their desired shaving experience.</p></p>
