<p><p>Whole-house ventilation solutions market includes various types of systems such as Exhaust Ventilation Systems, Supply Ventilation Systems, Balanced Ventilation Systems, and Energy Recovery Systems. Exhaust Ventilation Systems remove stale air from the house, while Supply Ventilation Systems bring in fresh air. Balanced Ventilation Systems provide a balanced combination of fresh and stale air exchange. Energy Recovery Systems are designed to improve energy efficiency by transferring heat or coolness between incoming and outgoing air streams. These systems cater to different needs and preferences for indoor air quality and energy efficiency.</p></p>
