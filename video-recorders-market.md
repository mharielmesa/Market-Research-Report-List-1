<p><p>The video recorder market consists of three main types: embedded DVRs, hybrid DVRs, and PC-based DVRs. Embedded DVRs are standalone devices that are designed to record and store video footage without the need for a separate computer. Hybrid DVRs combine the features of embedded DVRs with additional capabilities, such as the ability to connect to IP cameras. PC-based DVRs rely on a computer system to operate and offer increased flexibility and scalability. Each type caters to different needs and preferences, providing options for various video recording requirements.</p></p>
