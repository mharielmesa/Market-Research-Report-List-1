<p><p>Axial thrust bearings are used to support axial loads and maintain the axial position of rotating shafts. There are two types of axial thrust bearings: Single-direction bearings and double-direction bearings. </p><p>Single-direction bearings can only accommodate axial loads in one direction and are commonly used in applications where thrust loads act in one direction only. </p><p>In contrast, double-direction bearings can handle axial loads in both directions and are suitable for applications with bi-directional thrust loads, such as in the case of rotating shafts that need to support forces acting from opposite directions.</p></p>
