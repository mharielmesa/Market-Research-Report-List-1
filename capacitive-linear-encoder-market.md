<p><p>A capacitive linear encoder is a type of position sensor that employs capacitance technology to provide highly accurate measurements of linear position changes. It works on the principle that the capacitance between two parallel conductive plates changes as their relative position changes. These changes in capacitance are then converted into electrical signals, which can be further processed and used for various applications such as robotic systems, machine tools, and coordinate measuring machines.</p><p>The market for capacitive linear encoders is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecast period. <p><strong>The Capacitive Linear Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axle Type</li><li>Shaft Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Capacitive Linear Encoder market consists of two major types: Axle Type and Shaft Type. In Axle Type encoders, the capacitance variation is directly proportional to the linear motion of the axle. These encoders are commonly used in applications where the linear displacement needs to be measured accurately. On the other hand, Shaft Type encoders are designed to measure angular displacement. They employ capacitive sensors that detect the changes in capacitance as the shaft rotates, enabling accurate measurement of angular position. Both types of encoders find extensive applications in various industries for precise positioning and control purposes.</p></p>
