<p><p>Steam dried fishmeal is a popular ingredient used in animal feed products due to its high protein content. There are three main types of steam dried fishmeal in the market: defatted fish meal, semi-skimmed fish meal, and whole fish meal. Defatted fish meal is obtained by removing the oil from the fish, resulting in a low-fat product. Semi-skimmed fish meal contains a moderate amount of fat, while whole fish meal includes both protein and fat, offering a well-balanced composition. These different types cater to various nutritional requirements in animal diets.</p></p>
