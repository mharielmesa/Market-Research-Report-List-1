<p><p>A wafer type butterfly valve is a type of valve that is typically used to regulate or control the flow of fluids through pipes. It consists of a disc that rotates on a shaft in the middle of the valve, allowing the fluid to pass through when the disc is parallel to the pipe's direction and blocking the flow when the disc is perpendicular to the pipe's direction. This type of valve is commonly used in industries such as water treatment, chemical processing, oil and gas, and power generation.</p><p>The wafer type butterfly valve market is experiencing significant growth, primarily driven by the increasing demand for these valves in various sectors.
