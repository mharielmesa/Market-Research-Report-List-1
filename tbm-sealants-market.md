<p><strong>The TBM Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tail Sealants</li><li>Main Drive Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TBM sealants can be categorized into tail sealants and main drive sealants market. Tail sealants are designed to seal and protect the rear portion of the tunnel boring machine to prevent water and debris from entering. Main drive sealants, on the other hand, are used to seal the main drive components of the TBM, such as the cutter head and drive motors, to ensure smooth operation and prevent any leaks or failures. Both types of sealants play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and durability of tunnel boring machines.</p></p>
