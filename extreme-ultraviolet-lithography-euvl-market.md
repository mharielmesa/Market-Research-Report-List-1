<p><p>Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography (EUVL) utilizes a light source that emits extremely short wavelengths of light to create intricate patterns on silicon wafers. The mirrors used in EUVL are crucial for reflecting and focusing the EUV light onto the wafer with high precision. The mask market for EUVL involves the production of masks with intricate patterns that are used to transfer the design onto the silicon wafer.
