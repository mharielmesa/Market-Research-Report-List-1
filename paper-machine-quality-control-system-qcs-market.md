<p><p>A Paper Machine Quality Control System (QCS) is used to monitor and control the quality of paper during its manufacturing process. The market types in this system are divided into two categories: Machine Direction (MD) and Cross-machine Direction (CD). Machine Direction market focuses on measurements and controls regarding the length direction of the paper, such as basis weight and moisture. Cross-machine Direction market, on the other hand, focuses on measurements and controls across the width of the paper, like caliper and moisture profiles. Both markets are essential for ensuring the overall quality and consistency of the paper produced.</p></p>
