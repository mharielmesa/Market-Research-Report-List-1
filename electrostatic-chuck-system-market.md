<p><ul><li>Colulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek(JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrostatic Chuck System market consists of two main types: Colulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Colulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks rely on the principle of electrostatic attraction to secure objects in place, using electric charges to generate a clamping force. On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks employ a combination of electrostatic forces and vacuum pressure to hold objects. Both types are crucial for industries requiring secure and precise positioning of objects during various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
