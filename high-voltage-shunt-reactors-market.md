<p><p>The high voltage shunt reactors market consists of two types: dry type and oil-immersed type. Dry type reactors are designed without any insulation liquid and are suitable for indoor applications due to their compact size and low maintenance requirements. On the other hand, oil-immersed reactors are filled with insulation oil, providing higher cooling efficiency and allowing for outdoor installations. These reactors are commonly used in power transmission and distribution systems to regulate and control voltage levels, ensuring a stable and efficient energy flow.</p></p>
