<p><p>A solder sucker, also known as a desoldering pump, is a tool used to remove solder from a joint or connection. <p><p>The solder sucker market consists of two main types: manual solder suckers and electric solder suckers. Manual solder suckers are operated by hand, requiring the user to create a vacuum by pressing a button or pulling a trigger to remove molten solder. On the other hand, electric solder suckers are powered by electricity and have a built-in vacuum pump that automatically removes the solder when the trigger is pressed. These different market types offer options for users to choose the solder sucker that best fits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
