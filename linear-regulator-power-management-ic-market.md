<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Linear Regulator Power Management IC market consists of two main types: Series Type and Shunt Type. In the Series Type market, the IC regulates voltage by controlling the current flow through a series pass element. This results in a stable output voltage that can be adjusted as per requirements. On the other hand, in the Shunt Type market, the IC regulates voltage by diverting excess current to a shunt element. It ensures a constant output voltage by maintaining a specified voltage difference between the input and output terminals.</p></p>
