<p><strong>The Medical Thermometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mercury-in-glass Thermometer</li><li>Digital Thermometers</li><li>Infrared Thermometers</li><li>Disposable Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical thermometer market consists of various types of thermometers used for measuring body temperature. Mercury-in-glass thermometers rely on the expansion of mercury to indicate temperature. Digital thermometers use electronic sensors and display the results on a digital screen. Infrared thermometers measure temperature without physical contact by detecting thermal radiation. Disposable thermometers are designed for single-use and typically use liquid crystal technology. These different types provide options for accurate and convenient temperature measurement in medical settings and cater to various preferences and needs of healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
