<p><p>The conveyor and conveying equipment market comprises various types of equipment used for transporting goods and materials in industries. Overhead conveyors are suspended from the ceiling, providing an elevated pathway for items. Pallet conveyors are designed specifically for handling palletized loads. Other types include screw conveyors, vibrating conveyors, and slat conveyors, each serving a specific purpose in material handling and transportation processes.</p></p>
