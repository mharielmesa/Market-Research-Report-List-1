<p><p>Compensation software market includes two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based compensation software is a system accessible through the internet, allowing users to manage compensation processes remotely. It offers benefits like flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises compensation software is installed and operated on the company's local servers. It provides in-house control over the software and data but is generally more expensive and requires regular maintenance. Both types serve the purpose of facilitating compensation management but differ in terms of deployment and operational aspects.</p></p>
