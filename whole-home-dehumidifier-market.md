<p><p>Whole-home dehumidifiers are available in two main types - ducted dehumidifiers and free-standing dehumidifiers. Ducted dehumidifiers are integrated into the home's existing HVAC system, extracting excess moisture from the air as it circulates through the ducts. Free-standing dehumidifiers are standalone units that can be placed in various locations throughout the home to target specific areas with high humidity levels. Both types of dehumidifiers offer effective solutions for controlling indoor humidity and improving overall indoor air quality.</p></p>
