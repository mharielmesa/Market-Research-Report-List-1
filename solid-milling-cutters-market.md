<p><p>Solid milling cutters are cutting tools used in milling machines for removing material from a workpiece. The market for solid milling cutters can be categorized into four types. Peripheral cutting edge milling cutters have cutting edges on the outer periphery, enabling them to perform both face and peripheral milling. End cutting edge milling cutters have cutting edges on the end face, suitable for plunge and ramp milling. Shank milling cutters have a shank for attaching to the machine tool, while neck parts milling cutters have a narrow section between the shank and cutting edge for improved access in milling operations.</p></p>
