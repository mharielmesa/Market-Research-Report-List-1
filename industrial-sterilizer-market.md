Steam sterilizers use high-pressure saturated steam, hot air sterilizers use heated air, UV sterilizers employ ultraviolet light, ethylene oxide sterilizers use a gas, infrared sterilizers use electromagnetic radiation, and ozone sterilizers utilize ozone gas.
