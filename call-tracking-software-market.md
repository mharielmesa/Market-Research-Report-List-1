<p><p>Call tracking software market is divided into two major types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software is installed and hosted on the user's own servers or infrastructure. It offers more control over the security and customization, but requires upfront investment and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is hosted and managed by the provider, accessible via the internet. It offers ease of use, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as there is no need for infrastructure maintenance. Users can access it remotely and benefit from regular updates and backups.</p></p>
