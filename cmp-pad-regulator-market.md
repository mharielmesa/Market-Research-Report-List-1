<p><p>Conventional Pad Conditioners are commonly used in the CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) process to maintain and optimize the performance of CMP polishing pads. On the other hand, CVD Diamond Pad Conditioners utilize diamond coatings on the conditioning pads for enhanced durability and effectiveness in removing defects on the CMP pads. Both types of pad regulators play a crucial role in ensuring consistent and efficient planarization processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
