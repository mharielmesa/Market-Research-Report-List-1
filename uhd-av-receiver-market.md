<p><p>An UHD AV Receiver, also known as an Ultra High-Definition Audio/Video Receiver, is a device used in home entertainment systems to enhance the audio and visual experience. UHD AV Receivers typically support high-resolution audio and video formats, including 4K Ultra HD and Dolby Atmos.</p><p>The UHD AV Receiver market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, driven by the increasing demand for high-quality home entertainment systems.
