<p><p>The Abrasive Waterjet Cutting Machine market is segmented into two main types: Cantilever and Gantry. </p><p>Cantilever machines have a single bridge that supports the cutting head, allowing it to move along the length of the material being cut. This design is ideal for smaller workpieces or when space is limited.</p><p>Gantry machines, on the other hand, have two bridges that support the cutting head. This configuration offers greater stability and allows for cutting larger and heavier materials.</p><p>Both types of machines utilize abrasive waterjets to cut materials with high precision, using a combination of water and abrasive materials propelled at high pressure.</p></p>
