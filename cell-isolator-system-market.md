<p><p>The Cell Isolator System market is divided into two types: Single Cell Isolation and Multicellular Isolation. Single Cell Isolation refers to the process of isolating individual cells from a heterogeneous population, allowing for the study of specific cell characteristics and functions. Conversely, Multicellular Isolation involves the isolation and culturing of intact multicellular organisms or tissues for research purposes. Both types of isolations play a crucial role in various scientific fields, such as cancer research, stem cell studies, and drug discovery.</p></p>
