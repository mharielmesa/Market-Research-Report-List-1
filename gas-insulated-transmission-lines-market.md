<p><p>Gas Insulated Transmission Lines (GIL) are high-voltage power transmission cables consisting of a multi-layered pipe filled with sulfur hexafluoride (SF6) gas. GILs offer numerous advantages such as higher capacity, reduced losses, compact size, and low electromagnetic interference. They are commonly used for transmitting electrical power over long distances, especially in urban areas where land availability is limited. </p><p>The future outlook of the Gas Insulated Transmission Lines market is promising.
