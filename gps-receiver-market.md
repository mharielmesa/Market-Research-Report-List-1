<p><p>The GPS receiver market is categorized into Differential Grade and Survey Grade types. Differential Grade receivers are commonly used for navigation purposes and provide accurate positioning within a few meters. They are affordable and ideal for general use. On the other hand, Survey Grade receivers offer highly precise and reliable measurements that are essential in professional surveying and mapping applications. They provide accuracy within millimeters and are often more expensive due to their advanced features and specialized functionality. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs and requirements of the user.</p></p>
