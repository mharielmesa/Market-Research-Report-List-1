<p><p>Engraver Printer market can be categorized into two types: Flat Printer and Roller Printer. A Flat Printer is a device designed for printing on flat surfaces such as metals, plastics, or glass sheets. It utilizes a flat printing bed to hold the material, ensuring precise and accurate printing. On the other hand, a Roller Printer is specifically used for cylindrical or curved objects such as bottles or tubes. It features a rotating roller mechanism that allows seamless and consistent printing on these rounded surfaces. Both types serve distinct purposes and cater to different printing requirements.</p></p>
