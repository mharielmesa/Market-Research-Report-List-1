<p><ul><li>Non-contact Radar Level Gauge</li><li>Guided Wave Radar Level Gauge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar Level Gauges are used for measuring levels of various substances in tanks or containers. There are two main types of Radar Level Gauges: Non-contact Radar Level Gauge and Guided Wave Radar Level Gauge. Non-contact Radar Level Gauges use electromagnetic waves to measure levels without physically touching the substance, while Guided Wave Radar Level Gauges use a probe that directly contacts the substance. Both types of Radar Level Gauges are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and water treatment for accurate and reliable level measurements.</p></p>
