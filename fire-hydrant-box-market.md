<p><p>Fire hydrant boxes are essential components of fire protection systems. The market for fire hydrant boxes can be classified into two types: underground hydrants and ground hydrants. Underground hydrants are installed below the ground surface and can be accessed by removing a cover. They are commonly found in urban areas and provide a convenient water source for firefighting operations. In contrast, ground hydrants are located above the ground and are more commonly used in rural areas. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring quick access to water during emergencies.</p></p>
