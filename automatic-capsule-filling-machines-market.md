<p><p>The automatic capsule filling machines market consists of three main types: semi-automatic, fully automatic, and manual machines. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for operations like capsule loading and filling. Fully automatic machines can perform all functions, including capsule separation, filling, and sealing, without human interference. Manual machines, on the other hand, are entirely operated manually and do not have any automation features. These machines cater to different manufacturing needs based on the level of automation required for capsule filling processes.</p></p>
