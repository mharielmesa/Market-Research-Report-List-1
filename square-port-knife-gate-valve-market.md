<p><p>Square port knife gate valves are available in two market types: metal seat and resilient seat. Metal seat valves are designed with a metal-to-metal contact between the gate and seat, ensuring a tight and reliable seal, especially in high-temperature and abrasive media applications. Resilient seat valves, on the other hand, use a soft rubber seat to provide an excellent sealing performance, making them suitable for applications with slurries, wastewater, or other fluids containing solids. Both types of valves offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
