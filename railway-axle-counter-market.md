<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The railway axle counter market consists of two main types: rail side axle counters and on-rail axle counters. Rail side axle counters are installed on the side of the railway track and use sensors to detect the passing axles of trains. They are typically less expensive and easier to install. On-rail axle counters, on the other hand, are mounted directly on the railway track and are more accurate in determining the presence of axles. They are generally more expensive and require professional installation. Both types of axle counters play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of railway operations.</p></p>
