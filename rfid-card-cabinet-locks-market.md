<p><strong>The RFID Card Cabinet Locks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Range Type</li><li>Short & Middle Range Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID card cabinet locks are categorized into two market types: long range type and short & middle range type. The long-range type operates using radio frequency identification technology that can read cards from a distance, typically up to several meters. This allows for convenient access without the need for physical contact. On the other hand, the short and middle-range type requires closer proximity for card reading, usually within a few centimeters. These locks provide a higher level of security and are commonly used in applications where physical contact is necessary for access.</p></p>
