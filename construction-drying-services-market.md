Dehumidification involves reducing humidity levels using dehumidifiers and is effective for overall moisture control. Desiccant Drying utilizes desiccant materials to absorb moisture from the air, especially useful in extreme humidity conditions. Structural Drying focuses on drying specific areas affected by water damage, typically using specialized equipment. Other methods may include air circulation, heat drying, or vacuum drying.
