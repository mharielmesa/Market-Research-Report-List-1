The EPDM market can be categorized into two types based on the polymerization process: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. In solution polymerization, the monomers are dissolved in a solvent to form the rubber, while in suspension polymerization, the monomers are suspended in a liquid medium and reacted to form the polymer. Both processes have their own advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
