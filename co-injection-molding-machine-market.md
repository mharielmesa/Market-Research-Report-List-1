<p><p>Co-Injection Molding Machine Market Types include manual, semi-automatic, and automatic machines. Manual machines require operators to manually control the entire molding process, including material feeding and mold opening. Semi-automatic machines involve operators controlling some parts of the process, while other tasks are automated, such as material feeding. Automatic machines are fully automated, with all aspects of the molding process controlled by computer systems. Each type offers different levels of operator involvement and automation, catering to different production requirements and efficiency levels.</p></p>
