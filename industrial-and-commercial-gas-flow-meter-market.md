<p><p>In the industrial and commercial gas flow meter market, two common types are diaphragm gas flow meters and rotary gas flow meters. Diaphragm gas flow meters use a flexible diaphragm to measure the flow rate of gas. They are generally used for low flow rates and offer high accuracy. On the other hand, rotary gas flow meters utilize a rotating mechanism to measure the flow rate of gas. They are more suitable for higher flow rates and offer durability and reliability. These two types of gas flow meters are widely used in various industrial and commercial applications for measuring gas flow.</p></p>
