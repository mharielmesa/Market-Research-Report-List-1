<p><p>A high-speed microcentrifuge is a laboratory instrument used for rapid sample processing and separation of biological molecules. High-speed microcentrifuges are widely used in various applications such as molecular biology, biochemistry, cell biology, and clinical diagnostics.</p><p>The global high-speed microcentrifuge market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
