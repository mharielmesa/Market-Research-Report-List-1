<p><p>The roller vibrator market consists of two main types: standalone vibrators and model vibrators. Standalone vibrators are independent devices that can be used on their own, without any additional equipment. They are typically used for various applications such as compacting concrete or settling bulk materials. On the other hand, model vibrators are specifically designed to be attached to larger machinery or equipment, such as trucks or construction vehicles. These model vibrators provide vibration functionality while being integrated into the existing structure or system.</p></p>
