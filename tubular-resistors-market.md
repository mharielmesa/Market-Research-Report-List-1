<p><p>Tubular resistors are passive electronic components that serve the purpose of restricting current flow in a circuit. They consist of a resistive material enclosed within a tubular ceramic or metal housing. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing demand for durable and reliable electronic components across industries such as telecommunications, automotive, aerospace, and defense.
