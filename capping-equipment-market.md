<p><p>The capping equipment market encompasses three main types: semi-automatic capping equipment, automatic capping equipment, and manual capping equipment. Semi-automatic capping equipment requires operators to manually place caps on containers and activate the capping process, offering increased efficiency compared to manual methods. Automatic capping equipment, on the other hand, eliminates the need for human intervention by automatically placing and tightening caps onto containers. Manual capping equipment involves entirely manual processes, requiring operators to manually tighten caps. These various types cater to different needs and production scales within the capping industry.</p></p>
