<p><p>The Intelligent Cash Drawer market comprises two main types: All-In-One and POS Touch Machine. All-In-One cash drawers are a comprehensive solution combining a cash drawer, receipt printer, and touchscreen display in a single device. They are ideal for businesses with limited counter space.
