<p><p>Continuous flow testers are devices used to measure and analyze the performance of products or systems. The market for continuous flow testers can be classified into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic continuous flow testers are self-contained and require minimal human intervention. They are capable of performing all test functions automatically, providing quick and efficient testing processes. On the other hand, semi-automatic continuous flow testers still require some level of human involvement for operation and control. While they offer automation for certain functions, they rely on human input for other aspects of the testing process.</p></p>
