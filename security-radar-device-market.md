<p><strong>The Security Radar Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Imaging Radar</li><li>Non-imaging Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Security Radar Device market can be classified into two main types: Imaging Radar and Non-imaging Radar. Imaging Radar utilizes advanced technologies like Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) and provides high-resolution images of the surroundings. This enables precise target identification and tracking. On the other hand, Non-imaging Radar focuses on detecting and tracking objects without generating detailed images. It is commonly used for applications requiring long-distance detection, such as perimeter surveillance. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing security measures, but they differ in terms of their capabilities and applications.</p></p>
