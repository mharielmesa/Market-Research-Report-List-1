<p><strong>The Common Mode Noise Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole</li><li>SMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Common mode noise filters are used to suppress electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits. They are available in two market types: Through Hole and Surface Mount Device (SMD). Through Hole filters have leads that are inserted into holes on a printed circuit board and soldered on the opposite side. They are suitable for applications that require a high level of reliability and durability. On the other hand, SMD filters are compact components that are directly mounted onto the surface of the circuit board using reflow soldering techniques. They are more commonly used in smaller and lighter electronic devices.</p></p>
