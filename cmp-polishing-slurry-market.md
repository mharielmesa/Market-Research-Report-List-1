<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) Polishing Slurry is used in the semiconductor industry for the planarization of wafer surfaces. The market for CMP Polishing Slurry can be categorized into three types: Alumina Slurry, Colloidal Silica Slurry, and Ceria Slurry. </p><p>Alumina Slurry contains aluminum oxide particles and is widely used for the planarization of silicon wafers. Colloidal Silica Slurry consists of amorphous silica particles suspended in a liquid medium and is commonly used for advanced applications such as copper and barrier CMP. Ceria Slurry contains cerium oxide particles and is employed for the CMP of specific materials like glass, optical components, and non-silicon compound semiconductors. These different types of CMP Polishing Slurry cater to diverse polishing requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
