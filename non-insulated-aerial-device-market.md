<p><p>A non-insulated aerial device, also known as a non-insulated bucket truck or non-insulated boom lift, is a type of vehicle-mounted equipment used to transport workers to elevated locations for various purposes, such as construction, maintenance, and repair activities. Unlike insulated aerial devices, non-insulated ones do not provide electrical insulation to protect workers from electric shocks, making them unsuitable for working in close proximity to electrical hazards.</p><p>The non-insulated aerial device market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. The increasing demand for aerial devices in construction and infrastructure development projects is one of the primary drivers of market growth. Non-insulated aerial devices provide a cost-effective solution for aerial work in settings where electrical hazards are not a concern.</p><p>Moreover, the rising adoption of non-insulated aerial devices in industries such as telecommunication, forestry, and utilities for various maintenance and inspection tasks is fueling market growth. These industries require elevated access equipment that does not require electrical insulation, thereby driving the demand for non-insulated aerial devices.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of lightweight and durable non-insulated aerial devices, further contributing to market growth. Manufacturers are focusing on incorporating features like better maneuverability, improved safety, and increased reach to cater to the evolving needs of end-users.</p><p>The market is also witnessing several trends, such as the integration of telematics systems in non-insulated aerial devices. Telematics systems provide real-time monitoring, asset tracking, and remote diagnostics capabilities, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p><p>Overall, the non-insulated aerial device market is poised to experience strong growth in the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 11.3%. The increasing demand from construction, infrastructure, and various other industries, along with technological advancements, will drive market expansion.</p></p>
