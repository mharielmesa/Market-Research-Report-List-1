<p><ul><li>TTL Loupes</li><li>Flip-up Loupes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical Binocular Loupes are available in two main types: TTL (Through-The-Lens) Loupes and Flip-up Loupes. TTL Loupes are designed with the optics built directly into the lens, providing a more compact and lightweight design. Flip-up Loupes, on the other hand, have optics that can be flipped up and out of the way when not in use. Both types offer magnification for precise medical procedures, but each has its own advantages depending on the specific needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
