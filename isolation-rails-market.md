<p><p>Isolation rails are used in hospital beds to provide support and prevent patients from falling off. There are two types of isolation rails: fixed spring isolation rails and adjustable spring isolation rails. Fixed spring isolation rails are permanently attached to the bed frame and cannot be adjusted by the user. On the other hand, adjustable spring isolation rails can be moved up or down to accommodate the patient's needs. Both types of isolation rails serve the same purpose of ensuring patient safety and preventing accidents.</p></p>
