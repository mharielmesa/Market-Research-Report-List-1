<p><p>A Bypass Isolation Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) is a device used to transfer electrical power between two separate power sources, such as a primary utility power source and a backup generator. It ensures a seamless and reliable power transfer, especially during critical situations such as power outages or maintenance.</p><p>The bypass isolation feature of this ATS allows for manual control of the power transfer process, providing added flexibility and safety. It enables maintenance or repair work to be performed on the ATS without interrupting the power supply to the load, as the load can be isolated from the power sources.</p><p>The market for Bypass Isolation Automatic Transfer Switches is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecast period, mainly driven by the increasing need for reliable power backup solutions across various industries. These two types cater to different needs and requirements when it comes to redundant power supply systems, providing options for various industries and businesses.</p></p>
