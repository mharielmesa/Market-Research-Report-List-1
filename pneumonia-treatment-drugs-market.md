<p><p>Pneumonia Treatment Drugs are medications used to treat pneumonia, which is an infection in the lungs caused by bacteria, viruses, or fungi. Antibiotics are the most commonly prescribed drugs for bacterial pneumonia, while antivirals are used for viral pneumonia. Antifungals are employed for fungal pneumonia, which is rarer. Additionally, antipyretics are administered to reduce fever associated with pneumonia.</p><p>The Pneumonia Treatment Drugs Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. The increasing prevalence of pneumonia globally is a major driver of market growth. Pneumonia is a leading cause of morbidity and mortality, particularly in children and older adults. The rising geriatric population, higher incidence of chronic respiratory diseases, and immunocompromised individuals further contribute to the demand for pneumonia treatment drugs.</p><p>Advancements in drug development and the introduction of novel therapeutic options have also fueled market growth.
