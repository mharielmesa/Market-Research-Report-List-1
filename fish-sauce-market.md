<p><p>Fish sauce is a popular condiment with two main market types: traditional and industrial fish sauce. Traditional fish sauce is made through a natural fermentation process, using anchovies and salt. It is often high in quality and preferred by those seeking authentic flavors. On the other hand, industrial fish sauce is mass-produced using chemical additives and less fish content, resulting in a milder taste. It is targeted towards consumers looking for convenience and affordability. Both types cater to different preferences and offer distinct culinary experiences.</p></p>
