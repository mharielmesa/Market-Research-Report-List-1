<p><p>The minimally invasive instrumentation market includes various types of instruments used in minimally invasive surgeries. Handheld instruments are manually-operated tools used for precise movements and tissue manipulation. Inflation systems are used to create space within body cavities. Cutter instruments are used for cutting and dissecting tissues. Guiding devices assist in navigating through the body during surgery. Electrosurgical devices utilize electrical energy for cutting, coagulating, and sealing of tissues. Auxiliary instruments are used for additional support during surgeries. These instruments collectively aid in performing minimally invasive procedures with reduced trauma and faster recovery.</p></p>
