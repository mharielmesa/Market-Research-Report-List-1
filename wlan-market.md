<p><ul><li>Internal WLAN</li><li>Authorized Visitor WLAN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Internal WLAN refers to a wireless local area network that is intended for use by employees within an organization. It is a secure network that allows employees to access company resources and communicate with each other wirelessly. Authorized Visitor WLAN, on the other hand, is a separate network specifically designed for temporary or guest users who are given limited access to the organization's network and resources. This ensures that visitors can connect to the internet while maintaining the security and integrity of the internal network.</p></p>
