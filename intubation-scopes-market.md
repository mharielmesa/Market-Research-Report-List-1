<p><p>Intubation scopes are medical devices used during intubation procedures to help visualize the airway. Rigid intubation scopes are made of rigid materials, allowing for direct visualization but with limited maneuverability. Flexible intubation scopes, on the other hand, are made of flexible materials, offering better maneuverability through narrow or curved airways. Both types of scopes play a crucial role in ensuring successful intubation procedures by providing clear visualization and facilitating the insertion of endotracheal tubes or other airway devices.</p></p>
