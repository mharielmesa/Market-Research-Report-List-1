<p><p>PPTC stands for Polymeric Positive Temperature Coefficient fuses, which are commonly used in electronic applications as overcurrent protection devices. These fuses work by self-resetting when the current exceeds its rated value, and automatically return to their initial low-resistance state once the fault condition is removed. PPTC fuses provide protection against overcurrent events such as short circuits and overload conditions. They are widely used in industries such as automotive, telecommunications, consumer electronics, and industrial equipment.</p><p>The future outlook for the PPTC fuses market is positive.
