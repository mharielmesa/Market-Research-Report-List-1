<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) lamp market includes two main types: UV mercury lamps and UV LED lamps. UV mercury lamps use mercury vapor to produce UV light and are widely used in various applications such as water treatment, air purification, and printing. On the other hand, UV LED lamps use light-emitting diodes to generate UV light, offering benefits like lower energy consumption, longer lifespan, and compact size. Both types of lamps play a crucial role in industries requiring UV light sources for disinfection, sterilization, and curing processes.</p></p>
