Real estate professionals needed efficient tools to facilitate virtual transactions and maintain business continuity.</p><p>There are several latest trends shaping the real estate transaction management software market. One prominent trend is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) capabilities into the software, enabling tasks like contract analysis, predictive analytics, and personalized recommendations.
