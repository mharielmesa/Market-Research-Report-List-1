<p><p>The Surgical Robotics System market consists of two main types: Open Surgery and Minimal Invasive surgery. On the other hand, Minimal Invasive surgery involves the use of specialized robotic systems that enable surgeons to perform procedures through small incisions, using robotic arms with precise control and enhanced visualization.
