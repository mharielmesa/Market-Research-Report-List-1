<p><p>The disc harrows market consists of three main types: single action, offset type, and double action. Single action disc harrows are designed to cut through tough soil, preparing it for planting. Offset type disc harrows have an offset design, with the discs arranged in a diagonal pattern, allowing efficient cutting and leveling. Double action disc harrows are equipped with two sets of discs, working simultaneously to break up soil and create a smooth seedbed. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for specific soil conditions and farming needs.</p></p>
