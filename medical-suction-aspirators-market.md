<p><p>The medical suction aspirators market includes two main types: wall mounted suction units and portable surgical aspirators. Wall mounted suction units are fixed devices that are typically installed in hospitals and clinics. They provide a continuous source of suction and are designed for larger medical facilities. On the other hand, portable surgical aspirators are compact and portable devices that can be easily moved around. They are commonly used in ambulances, home healthcare settings, and smaller medical facilities. Both types of aspirators are essential for removing fluids and debris from the body during medical procedures.</p></p>
