<p><ul><li>Sheet Linoleum Flooring</li><li>Linoleum Tile Flooring</li><li>Floating Linoleum Floors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linoleum resilient flooring market comprises three main types: sheet linoleum flooring, linoleum tile flooring, and floating linoleum floors. Sheet linoleum flooring is manufactured in large sheets and then cut to fit the intended space, offering a seamless and easy-to-clean surface. Linoleum tile flooring, as the name suggests, consists of individual tiles that can be easily installed and replaced. Lastly, floating linoleum floors are composed of interlocking planks that "float" above the subfloor, providing a quick and convenient installation method without the need for adhesives.</p></p>
