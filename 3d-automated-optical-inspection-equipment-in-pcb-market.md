<p><p>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment market for PCB is divided into two types: inline 3D AOI and offline 3D AOI. Inline 3D AOI systems are integrated into the PCB assembly line, conducting real-time inspections as the PCBs move through the production process. It ensures faster inspection and immediate feedback to address any errors. On the other hand, offline 3D AOI systems operate separately, allowing for more in-depth analysis and higher inspection accuracy. This type is suitable for post-production analysis and quality control purposes.</p></p>
