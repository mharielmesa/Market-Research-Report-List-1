One-dimensional optical position sensors detect position along a single axis, commonly used in linear position sensing applications. Two-dimensional optical position sensors determine position in two axes, often used in joystick or touchpad applications. Multi-axial optical position sensors can detect position in multiple axes simultaneously, suitable for applications such as robotics or gaming controllers.</p></p>
