<p><strong>The Passenger Boarding Bridge (PBB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Movable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger Boarding Bridges (PBB) come in two main types: Fixed and Movable. Fixed PBBs are permanently attached to the terminal building, providing a direct and permanent connection between the building and the aircraft. Movable PBBs, on the other hand, can be easily repositioned to accommodate various aircraft sizes and types. They offer more flexibility in terms of gate assignment and can be shared among multiple gates. Both types of PBBs play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient passenger boarding and disembarkation processes at airports.</p></p>
