<p><p>The Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) Diagnostics market consists of three main types: CTC Enrichment, CTC Detection, and CTC Analysis. CTC Enrichment involves isolating CTCs from the blood sample. CTC Detection involves identifying and locating CTCs using various techniques such as immunofluorescence or polymerase chain reaction (PCR). CTC Analysis focuses on characterizing and evaluating CTCs for genetic or molecular analysis, providing insights into prognosis and treatment response. These three types work together to enable the detection, diagnosis, and monitoring of cancer progression through the analysis of CTCs in the patient's bloodstream.</p></p>
