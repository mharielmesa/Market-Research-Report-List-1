<p><strong>The Orthodontic Headgear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cervical Pull</li><li>High-Pull Headgear</li><li>Reverse-Pull (Facemask)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthodontic headgear is a device used in orthodontic treatment to correct misalignment of teeth and jaws. The three main types are cervical pull, high-pull headgear, and reverse-pull (facemask). Cervical pull headgear applies force to the back of the head, while high-pull headgear applies force to both the back of the head and the upper jaw. Reverse-pull headgear, also known as a facemask, applies force to the forehead and chin, encouraging forward growth of the upper jaw. These types of headgear play a crucial role in aligning the teeth and achieving proper bite alignment.</p></p>
