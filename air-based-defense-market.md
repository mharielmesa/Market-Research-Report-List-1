<p><p>Air-based defense refers to the use of various air defense systems and strategies to protect a country or organization from aerial threats. The primary objective of air-based defense is to detect, track, engage, and neutralize or destroy these threats before they can inflict damage.</p><p>The market for air-based defense has been witnessing significant growth in recent years. These technologies enable faster and more accurate detection of aerial threats, allowing for quicker response times and minimizing the risk of successful attacks. Integration of these systems with command and control networks also enables better coordination and information sharing among various defense systems.</p><p>Another trend is the development of anti-UAV defense systems. Anti-UAV systems use a combination of radar, sensors, and jammers to detect, track, and neutralize hostile UAVs.</p><p>The market growth analysis suggests that the air-based defense market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period.
