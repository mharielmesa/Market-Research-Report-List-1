<p><p>Oscilloscope probes are used to measure electrical signals and are essential tools for electronic testing and design. The market for oscilloscope probes can be categorized into three types: passive probes, active probes, and other specialized probes. </p><p>Passive probes are the most common type and rely on simple components to transfer the signal from the device under test to the oscilloscope. Active probes, on the other hand, incorporate active circuitry to provide features like better impedance matching and lower loading effect. Other specialized probes include high-voltage probes, differential probes, and current probes, which are designed for specific measurement needs.</p></p>
