<p><p>Standard and Hammer mills are both types of straw mills used for processing straw into smaller particles. The standard market type employs fixed hammers that rotate around a central axle, continuously striking the straw and breaking it down. On the other hand, the hammer mill type utilizes a combination of fixed and swinging hammers to pulverize the straw more efficiently. Both types serve the purpose of reducing the size of straw particles, but the hammer mill offers greater versatility and faster processing due to its swinging hammers.</p></p>
