<p><p>The Immune Thrombocytopenia Drugs Market consists of various types of drugs used to treat immune thrombocytopenia, a condition characterized by low platelet count. Thrombopoietin Receptor Agonists (TPO-RAs) are drugs that stimulate the production of platelets in the bone marrow. Corticosteroids, such as prednisone, reduce inflammation and prevent the destruction of platelets by the immune system. Intravenous Immunoglobins (IVIGs) are antibodies derived from blood plasma that help regulate the immune response. Other drugs may include rituximab, danazol, and azathioprine, which also work to suppress the immune system and increase platelet production.</p></p>
