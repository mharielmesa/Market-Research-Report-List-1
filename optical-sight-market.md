<p><p>The Optical Sight market comprises two prominent types, namely Image Intensifier and Thermal Imaging. Image intensifier sights are optical devices that amplify existing light to provide enhanced vision in low-light conditions. They capture faint light and electronically amplify it for clear visibility. On the other hand, Thermal Image sights detect and capture heat signatures emitted by objects, allowing users to see in complete darkness or through smoke and haze. These technologies have various applications, including military, law enforcement, surveillance, and hunting, to improve visibility and target acquisition capabilities.</p></p>
