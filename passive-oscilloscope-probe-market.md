<p><p>Passive oscilloscope probe market types include 1×, 10×, 100×, and others. The 1× probe provides a direct connection between the circuit and the oscilloscope, offering a true representation of the signal but with reduced bandwidth. The 10× probe attenuates the signal by a factor of 10, reducing the loading effect on the circuit and improving accuracy. The 100× probe further attenuates the signal, suitable for high voltage measurements. The "others" category encompasses probes with specialized features like differential or high-frequency capabilities, meeting specific measurement needs.</p></p>
