<p><p>Fiber laser modules for LiDAR technology are available in two main types: long pulsed and short pulsed. Long pulsed fiber lasers emit continuous pulses of light, which are ideal for applications requiring high power levels and longer pulse durations. Short pulsed fiber lasers, on the other hand, emit pulses of light with very short durations, making them suitable for high precision applications such as LiDAR systems. Both types of fiber laser modules play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and accuracy of LiDAR technology.</p></p>
