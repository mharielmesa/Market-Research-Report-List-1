<p><p>Rubber additives refer to various chemicals and materials used in the rubber industry to enhance the performance and properties of rubber products. The market for rubber additives can be segmented into several types. Firstly, rubber antioxidants are used to prevent the degradation of rubber materials caused by oxidation, thereby extending the product's lifespan. Secondly, rubber accelerators are substances that promote a faster vulcanization process, resulting in improved strength and elasticity of rubber products. Insoluble sulfur is another type of rubber additive that enhances the heat resistance of rubber materials. Lastly, the "Other" category includes various additives such as adhesion promoters, plasticizers, and blowing agents that serve different purposes in the rubber industry.</p></p>
