Short PIVCs are more commonly used in hospitals for temporary access, while Integrated/Closed PIVCs are preferred for long-term treatments to prevent needlestick injuries and contamination. Both types cater to different needs in the healthcare industry and offer unique benefits for patients and healthcare providers.</p></p>
