<p><ul><li>Mechanical Crack Meters</li><li>Wire Crack Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical crack meters are devices used to measure any movement or displacement in buildings or structures. They consist of mechanical gauges and sensors that help in monitoring cracks. These meters are economical and easy to install, making them suitable for small-scale applications. On the other hand, wire crack meters use linear displacement transducers or extensometers to measure crack openings. These meters provide accurate measurements and are ideal for long-term monitoring of cracks in large structures or infrastructure projects. Both mechanical and wire crack meters play a crucial role in ensuring the stability and safety of buildings and structures.</p></p>
