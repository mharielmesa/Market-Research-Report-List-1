<p><p>Sewage pumps are used to transport wastewater or sewage from residential or industrial areas to a treatment facility. Day sewage pumps are designed to be manually operated and are suitable for smaller applications. They typically have a smaller capacity and are cost-effective options. On the other hand, submersible sewage pumps are designed to be fully submerged in the sewage or wastewater. They are more powerful and able to handle larger volumes of sewage, making them ideal for larger applications or situations where continuous operation is required.</p></p>
