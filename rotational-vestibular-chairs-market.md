<p><ul><li>Manual Wholebody Rotation Chair</li><li>Passive Headon-body Rotation Chair</li><li>Active Headon-body Rotation Chair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotational vestibular chair market comprises three types: manual whole-body rotation chair, passive head-on-body rotation chair, and active head-on-body rotation chair. The manual whole-body rotation chair is manually operated by the user to rotate the entire body. The passive head-on-body rotation chair uses external mechanisms to rotate the user's head on their body. In contrast, the active head-on-body rotation chair, also known as a dynamic chair, incorporates automated technologies to rotate the user's head on their body. These chairs are used to assess and treat vestibular system disorders.</p></p>
