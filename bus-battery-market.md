<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bus Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFP</li><li>NMC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bus battery market consists of two main types: LFP and NMC. LFP, which stands for lithium iron phosphate, is a type of lithium-ion battery. It is known for its long cycle life, good thermal stability, and high safety performance. On the other hand, NMC, or lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide, is another type of lithium-ion battery. It offers a higher energy density, allowing for longer driving range, but may have slightly lower cycle life and safety performance compared to LFP batteries. Both LFP and NMC batteries are commonly used in electric buses due to their specific characteristics.</p></p>
