<p><p>The polymerase chain reaction (PCR) technology is used in digital forensics to amplify and detect specific DNA sequences. Capillary electrophoresis is employed to separate and analyze DNA fragments based on their size and charge. Next-generation sequencing aids in the high-throughput analysis of DNA, enabling the identification of genetic mutations or variations. Rapid DNA analysis offers quick on-site DNA testing, allowing for immediate identification of individuals. These technologies play a crucial role in digital forensics by providing accurate and efficient analysis of DNA evidence in criminal investigations.</p></p>
