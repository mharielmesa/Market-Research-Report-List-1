<p><p>Energy Recovery Ventilators (ERVs) are devices used to improve indoor air quality and reduce energy consumption in buildings. There are different types of ERVs available in the market. Rotary enthalpy wheels use a rotating wheel to transfer heat and moisture between the incoming and outgoing air streams. Fixed plate ERVs use multiple plates to transfer heat but not moisture. Other types include heat pipe and run-around coil ERVs. These devices help maintain a comfortable and healthy indoor environment while recovering and reusing energy that would have been lost during ventilation.</p></p>
