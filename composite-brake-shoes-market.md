Firstly, the rise in vehicle production and the growing demand for automobiles worldwide are driving the demand for composite brake shoes. As the automotive industry focuses on enhancing vehicle safety and performance, the adoption of advanced braking systems, including composite brake shoes, is increasing.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing emphasis on reducing vehicle weight and improving fuel efficiency is also boosting the demand for composite brake shoes. This reduction in weight contributes to overall vehicle weight reduction, leading to improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing the emergence of innovative composite materials with enhanced characteristics, such as higher friction coefficients and better heat dissipation abilities. <p><p>Composite brake shoes are widely used in the automotive industry. They are available in two main types: synthetic resin base and rubber base.
