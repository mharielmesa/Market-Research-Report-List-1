<p><p>The Disposable Coin Li-ion Battery market can be categorized into four types: LR, SR, CR, and ZA. LR batteries stand for "low drain," designed for devices with minimal power requirements like watches. SR batteries, or "silver oxide," are commonly used in medical devices due to their stable voltage. CR batteries, known as "button cells," are suitable for high-drain devices like cameras. Lastly, ZA batteries, or "zinc-air," are used for hearing aids as they provide long-lasting power. These market types differentiate the batteries based on their intended applications and power capabilities.</p></p>
