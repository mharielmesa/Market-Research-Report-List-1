<p><p>The propane refrigeration compressor market consists of two main types: piston compressors and screw compressors. </p><p>Piston compressors use a piston-cylinder arrangement to compress the refrigerant gas. They are commonly used in small and medium-sized refrigeration systems due to their compact size and simple design. </p><p>On the other hand, screw compressors utilize two interlocking helical screws to compress the gas. They are known for their high efficiency and larger capacity, making them suitable for large-scale industrial refrigeration applications.</p></p>
