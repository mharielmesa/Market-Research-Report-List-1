<p><p>The Hot Air System Market encompasses various types of heating systems. Central Heat refers to a system that distributes heat from a central furnace or boiler throughout a building, typically through ductwork. Direct Heat involves heating a space directly using sources like stoves or radiant heaters. Hybrid Heating combines multiple heating sources, such as a heat pump and a furnace, for increased energy efficiency. Ground Source Heat Pump uses the earth's natural heat to provide heating, cooling, and hot water by utilizing pipes buried underground.</p></p>
