<p><p>Poly-woven dunnage air bags are used to secure cargo during transportation. They come in different types based on the number of layers they have, typically ranging from 2 to 8 ply. The ply refers to the number of layers of poly-woven fabric used to construct the air bag. Generally, higher ply counts indicate stronger and more durable air bags that provide better protection against shifting cargo. 2 ply bags are usually used for lighter loads, while 4 ply and 6 ply bags are suitable for medium-weight loads. 8 ply bags are designed for heavier loads, ensuring maximum stability and hold.</p></p>
