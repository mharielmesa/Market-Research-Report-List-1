<p><strong>The Centrifugal Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Centrifugal Compressors</li><li>Multi-stage Centrifugal Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal compressors are used in various industries for compressing gases. Single-stage centrifugal compressors have a single impeller that rotates at high speeds to compress the gas in a single stage. They are suitable for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, multi-stage centrifugal compressors have multiple impellers, each compressing the gas further in successive stages. They are used for high-pressure applications where greater compression ratios are required. The centrifugal compressors market includes both single-stage and multi-stage compressors, catering to different pressure requirements in different industries.</p></p>
