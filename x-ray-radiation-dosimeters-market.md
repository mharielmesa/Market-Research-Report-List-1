These dosimeters come in various types, including TFT (thin-film transistor), CMOS (complementary metal-oxide semiconductor), and Others. TFT dosimeters utilize thin-film transistor technology to detect and measure X-ray radiation levels. CMOS dosimeters adopt complementary metal-oxide semiconductor sensors for radiation detection. Other types may include alternative technologies or variations of the TFT and CMOS. Each type offers different features and capabilities, allowing users to choose the most suitable dosimeter for their specific needs.</p></p>
