<p><p>The Rapid Antigen Test Kit market includes various types of tests used for diagnosing diseases. Immunosorbent Assay detects specific antigens in a sample using antibodies. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay uses enzymes to detect antigens. Precipitation Reaction causes antigen-antibody complexes to aggregate. Radioimmunoassay uses radioactive tracers to measure antigens. Agglutination Reaction clumps antigens together. Complement Fixation determines the level of complement proteins in a sample. These tests aid in the timely and accurate diagnosis of diseases, allowing for effective treatment and containment of infections.</p></p>
