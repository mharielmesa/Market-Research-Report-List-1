<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering Systems come in different types based on their mechanisms. C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering) utilizes an electric motor mounted on the steering column. P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering) has the motor mounted on the steering gear. R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering) features the motor located on the rack and pinion assembly. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of control, efficiency, and performance, catering to the diverse needs of the automotive market.</p></p>
