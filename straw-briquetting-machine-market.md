<p><p>The straw briquetting machine market can be categorized into three types: mechanical, hydraulic, and screw extrusion. </p><p>Mechanical straw briquetting machines use mechanical force to compress straw into briquettes. These machines are typically simple and easy to operate, making them ideal for small-scale operations. </p><p>Hydraulic straw briquetting machines utilize hydraulic pressure to compress straw into briquettes. They offer higher production capacity and can handle larger volumes of straw. </p><p>Screw extrusion straw briquetting machines use a screw to compress straw into briquettes. They are known for their efficiency and versatility, as they can process various types of straws and produce briquettes of different shapes and sizes.</p></p>
