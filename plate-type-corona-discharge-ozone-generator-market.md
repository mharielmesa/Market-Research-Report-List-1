They are commonly used in home appliances, air purifiers, and small-scale water treatment systems due to their low power consumption and compact size. </p><p>Medium Frequency generators operate at frequencies between 100 kHz and 1 MHz, employing wider and more closely spaced electrodes. They are typically used in larger industrial applications, such as water treatment plants and food processing facilities, as they provide higher concentration and more efficient ozone production.</p></p>
