<p><p>Fault circuit indicators are devices used in electrical power systems to detect and indicate fault or abnormal conditions in the network. The market for fault circuit indicators can be categorized into four main types. Overhead line fault circuit indicators are installed on overhead power lines and monitor faults occurring in this configuration. Cable fault circuit indicators are used to detect faults in underground cables. Panel fault circuit indicators are mounted on equipment panels to indicate faults within the panel. Lastly, there are other types of fault circuit indicators available that cater to specific needs or unique installations within the power system.</p></p>
