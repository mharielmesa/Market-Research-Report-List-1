<p><p>Fertility sensors are devices utilized by women to track their ovulation cycles and determine their fertile days for conception. Different types of fertility sensors are available in the market. Luteinizing Hormone (LH) monitors measure the levels of LH in urine to predict ovulation. Thermal monitors track basal body temperature variations that occur during the menstrual cycle. Electrolyte monitors analyze saliva or sweat to detect hormone changes. Besides these three, there are also other fertility sensors that employ various methods, such as tracking cervical mucus and measuring hormonal fluctuations through wearable devices.</p></p>
