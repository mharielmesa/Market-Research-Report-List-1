<p><p>The hops market can be classified into three main types: bittering hops, aroma hops, and dual-purpose hops. Bittering hops are primarily used to add bitterness to beer during the brewing process, imparting a distinct taste. Aroma hops, on the other hand, are known for their aromatic properties, giving beer a pleasant and fragrant smell. Dual-purpose hops, as the name suggests, possess both bittering and aromatic qualities, making them a versatile choice for brewers who want to balance bitterness and aroma in their beer recipes.</p></p>
