<p><p>Proportional solenoid valves are classified into three main types for different functions. Pressure control valves regulate the pressure of fluids within a system, ensuring it stays within desired limits. Flow control valves manage and regulate the flow rate of fluids through a system. Directional control valves determine the direction of flow within a system, steering fluids between different fluid paths. These valves are used in various industries to maintain optimal fluid pressure, flow, and direction, enhancing system efficiency and performance.</p></p>
