<p><p>The bottle cleaning machines market consists of three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully-automatic. Manual machines require human intervention for all cleaning tasks, making them suitable for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic machines automate some aspects of the cleaning process while still requiring some level of human involvement. These are commonly used in medium-sized production facilities. Fully-automatic machines can perform all cleaning tasks without human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale operations with high production volumes.</p></p>
