<p><p>The electric fence monitor market can be categorized into three types based on voltage levels: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage fence monitors. Low voltage fence monitors typically operate at voltage levels below 1,000 volts and are suitable for residential applications. Medium voltage fence monitors operate at voltage levels between 1,000 and 10,000 volts, making them ideal for commercial and agricultural use. High voltage fence monitors operate at voltage levels above 10,000 volts and are primarily used for industrial or military purposes where higher levels of security are required.</p></p>
