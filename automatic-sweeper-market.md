<p><p>Automatic sweeper market types include the infrared sensor type and ultrasonic bionic type. The infrared sensor type utilizes infrared sensors to detect obstacles and navigate accordingly, ensuring efficient cleaning without colliding with objects. On the other hand, the ultrasonic bionic type uses ultrasonic technology to mimic the behavior of insects, attracting dust and cleaning it effectively. Both types of automatic sweepers offer convenience and efficiency in cleaning tasks, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
