<p><p>The interlocking nails market consists of two main types: biomaterials and metallic implants. Metallic implants, on the other hand, are made from metals such as stainless steel or titanium and provide structural support and stability to the fractured bones. Both types of interlocking nails are widely used in orthopedic surgeries to treat fractures and help patients recover mobility and function.</p></p>
