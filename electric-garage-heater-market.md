<p><strong>The Electric Garage Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-forced</li><li>Infrared</li><li>Ceramic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric garage heater market includes three types: fan-forced, infrared, and ceramic heaters. Fan-forced heaters use a fan to blow air over a heating element, evenly distributing warmth throughout the garage. Infrared heaters emit infrared radiation that heats objects and people directly, rather than warming the air. Ceramic heaters utilize ceramic plates and aluminum baffles to create and distribute heat in the surrounding area. Each type offers different benefits and features, allowing consumers the flexibility to choose the most suitable option based on their specific heating needs and preferences.</p></p>
