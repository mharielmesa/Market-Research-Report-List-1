<p><p>Big Data as a Service (BDaaS) refers to the outsourcing of big data analysis and processing needs by organizations. In contrast, cloud-based BDaaS leverages the resources of third-party service providers, offering scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. Organizations can access the necessary big data tools and services remotely, without the need for infrastructure maintenance, allowing them to focus on their core business activities.</p></p>
