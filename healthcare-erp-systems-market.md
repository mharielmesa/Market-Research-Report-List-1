<p><p>Healthcare ERP systems refer to enterprise resource planning systems designed specifically for the healthcare industry. These systems are classified into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based healthcare ERP systems operate on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage data through the internet. On the other hand, on-premises systems are installed and run locally on the organization's own servers and infrastructure. While cloud-based systems offer flexibility, scalability, and reduced costs, on-premises systems provide greater control, security, and customization options.</p></p>
