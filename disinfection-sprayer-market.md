<p><strong>The Disinfection Sprayer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrostatic</li><li>Non-Electrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disinfection Sprayer market comprises two main types: Electrostatic and Non-Electrostatic sprayers. Electrostatic sprayers use electrical charges to disperse the disinfectant particles, resulting in enhanced adherence to surfaces and a more even distribution. They are commonly used in large-scale applications, such as commercial spaces and healthcare facilities. On the other hand, Non-Electrostatic sprayers distribute the disinfectant through air pressure or mechanical means. While they may not provide the same level of coverage as electrostatic sprayers, they are more affordable and suitable for smaller areas like homes or offices.</p></p>
