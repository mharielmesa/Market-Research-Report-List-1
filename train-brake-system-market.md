<p><p>The train brake system market consists of two main types: brakeshoe brake system and disc brake system. Brakeshoe brake system is the traditional and widely used method, where a brake shoe is pressed against the train wheel to create friction and slow down or stop the train. On the other hand, the disc brake system uses a disc and calipers to generate friction on both sides of the disc, providing better stopping power and heat dissipation. Both systems play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of train operations.</p></p>
