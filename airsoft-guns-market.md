<p><strong>The Airsoft Guns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring-powered</li><li>Battery-powered</li><li>Gas-powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airsoft guns are available in three main market types based on their power source: spring-powered, battery-powered, and gas-powered. Spring-powered airsoft guns require manual cocking of the spring before each shot, making them less powerful but more reliable. Battery-powered airsoft guns use rechargeable batteries to power an electric motor for automatic firing capabilities. Gas-powered airsoft guns utilize compressed gas such as CO2 or green gas for high-powered shots with realistic blowback action. Each market type offers different advantages and caters to different preferences among airsoft enthusiasts.</p></p>
