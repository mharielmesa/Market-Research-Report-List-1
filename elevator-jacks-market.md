<p><p>There are two main types of elevator jacks in the market: Standard Holed Jacks and Customized Holed Jacks. Standard Holed Jacks are the most commonly used jacks and are designed to fit a wide range of elevator systems. They have pre-drilled holes that align with standard elevator rails, making installation easier. On the other hand, Customized Holed Jacks are specifically designed to meet unique specifications or requirements of certain elevator systems. These jacks are made-to-order and have custom hole placements to fit the specific elevator rail designs.</p></p>
