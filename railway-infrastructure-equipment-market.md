<p><strong>The Railway Infrastructure Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sub-Structure</li><li>Super Structure</li><li>Special Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway infrastructure equipment market can be categorized into three main types: sub-structure, super structure, and special structure. Sub-structure refers to the foundation and support systems of railway tracks, including ballasts, sleepers, and drainage systems. Super structure includes rails, switches, and crossings that facilitate the movement of trains. Special structure encompasses equipment such as tunnels, bridges, and viaducts, which are essential for railway operations over challenging terrain or water bodies. These three categories cover the different components and systems necessary for establishing and maintaining a functional railway network.</p></p>
