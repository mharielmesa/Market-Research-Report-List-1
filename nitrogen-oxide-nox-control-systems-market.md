Governments around the world are focusing on limiting the emission of NOx from industrial processes and vehicles, leading to a surge in the demand for effective control systems.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness among industries and individuals about the harmful effects of NOx emissions on human health and the environment has further propelled the market growth.
