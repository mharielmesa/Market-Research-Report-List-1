<p><ul><li>Electro-Hydraulic Actuator</li><li>Electro-Mechanical Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Actuator System Market consists of different types of actuator systems, including the Electro-Hydraulic Actuator and the Electro-Mechanical Actuator. </p><p>The Electro-Hydraulic Actuator system combines the advantages of hydraulics and electronics, using hydraulic fluid and electrical signals to convert energy and generate motion. This type of actuator is widely used in commercial and military aircraft due to its high power density and reliability. </p><p>On the other hand, the Electro-Mechanical Actuator system utilizes electric motors to generate motion without the need for hydraulic fluids. It offers advantages such as energy efficiency, precise control, and reduced maintenance requirements. This actuator type is commonly used in flight control surfaces and thrust reversers in aircraft.</p></p>
