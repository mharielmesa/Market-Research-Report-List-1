<p><p>Non Destructive Testing (NDT) in the Aerospace & Defense market involves the use of various techniques to inspect and evaluate materials and components without causing any damage. Ultrasonic testing uses sound waves to detect internal defects, while radiography testing relies on X-rays or gamma rays to examine the interior structures. Magnetic particle testing uses magnetic fields to identify surface cracks, and eddy-current testing detects surface defects through electromagnetic induction. Other NDT techniques may include visual inspection, liquid penetrant testing, and infrared thermography, all aiming to ensure the reliability and safety of aerospace and defense systems.</p></p>
