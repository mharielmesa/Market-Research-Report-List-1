<p><strong>The RTLS for Industrial Applications Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Systems</li><li>Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) are used in industrial applications to track and locate assets or people within a facility. This technology is implemented through various types of systems, such as Wi-Fi-based, Ultra-Wideband (UWB), Infrared (IR), or Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID), which enable real-time monitoring of the precise location of objects. Tags, on the other hand, are small devices attached to assets or worn by individuals to transmit location data to the RTLS system. These tags can be active (battery-powered) or passive (requiring an external source to activate).</p></p>
