<p><p>Label Adhesives market is segmented into three main types - Water-based, Hot Melt-based, and Solvent-based adhesives. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly and safe, making them ideal for food packaging. Hot Melt-based adhesives offer fast bonding and high initial tack suitable for high-speed labeling applications. Solvent-based adhesives provide excellent adhesion to a variety of substrates and can withstand harsh conditions. Each type of adhesive has its unique properties and applications, catering to a diverse range of labeling needs in different industries.</p></p>
