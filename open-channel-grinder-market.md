<p><ul><li>Single Drum</li><li>Dual Drums</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open channel grinders are widely used in wastewater treatment plants to reduce the size of solids in sewage, facilitating their transportation and downstream processes. Single drum open channel grinders comprise a single rotating drum, while dual drums feature two rotating drums. Single drum grinders are typically more compact and cost-effective, suitable for smaller treatment plants with less demanding solids processing requirements. Dual drum grinders, on the other hand, provide higher capacity and enhanced performance, making them suitable for larger treatment plants with higher solids volumes or more challenging wastewater conditions.</p></p>
