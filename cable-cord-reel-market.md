<p><p>The cable cord reel market is divided into two types: electric reel and manual reel. Electric reel refers to a type of reel that automatically rewinds the cable cord using an electric motor, usually operated with a push-button or remote control. This type offers convenience and ease of use, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, manual reels require physical effort to rewind the cord, usually by manually cranking a handle. Manual reels are typically more affordable but are better suited for lighter applications or occasional use.</p></p>
