<p><p>Automatic hermetic sliding doors are widely used in various settings such as hospitals, laboratories, and cleanrooms for their ability to maintain the integrity of sterile environments. These doors are available in two main types: single-leaf and double-leaf. Single-leaf doors consist of a single sliding panel, suitable for smaller entrances. Double-leaf doors feature two panels that slide open simultaneously, making them suitable for wider entrances or areas with higher traffic. Both types provide airtight seals when closed, preventing the transfer of contaminants and maintaining sterility within the controlled environment they serve.</p></p>
