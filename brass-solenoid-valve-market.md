<p><p>Brass solenoid valves are widely used for controlling the flow of various fluids in many industries. There are two main types of market configurations for these valves: Normally Open and Normally Closed. Normally Open valves allow fluid to flow through when de-energized, and close the passage when energized. Conversely, Normally Closed valves block fluid flow when de-energized and open the passage when energized. These configurations offer different advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application, allowing for efficient fluid control in various industries.</p></p>
