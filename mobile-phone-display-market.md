<p><p>The mobile phone display market consists of two major types: LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) and OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode). LCD displays use liquid crystals to create an image, relying on a backlight to illuminate the pixels. OLED displays, on the other hand, emit light individually through organic compounds, eliminating the need for a backlight and providing deeper blacks and richer colors. Both technologies have their advantages and disadvantages, such as LCD offering better outdoor visibility and lower production costs, while OLED provides higher contrast and better energy efficiency.</p></p>
