<p><p>Non-spring return damper actuators are devices used in HVAC systems to control the position of dampers, which regulate the flow of air in ducts. Unlike spring return actuators that use a spring mechanism to return to a default position, non-spring return actuators rely on electric motors. These actuators are widely used in commercial and residential buildings to improve energy efficiency and enhance indoor air quality.</p><p>The non-spring return damper actuators market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. Three-Position Control actuators provide an additional intermediate position within the damper's range of movement.
