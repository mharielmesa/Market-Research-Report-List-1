<p><p>Personal fall prevention equipment refers to a range of safety devices designed to protect individuals working at heights. Safety harnesses are worn around the body and connected to a secure anchor point, while safety lanyards are attached to the harness and prevent falls by creating a secure connection. Anchorage connectors are used to create secure attachment points for the harnesses and lanyards. Fall arresters are devices that activate in the event of a fall, arresting the person's movement. Lifeline systems provide continuous protection by allowing workers to move along a secure line. Other equipment may include self-retracting lifelines, body belts, and rope grabs.</p></p>
