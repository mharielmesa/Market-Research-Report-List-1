<p><ul><li>Incremental Shaft Encoders</li><li>Absolute Shaft Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical shaft encoders are devices used to measure the position, speed, and direction of rotation of a shaft. Incremental shaft encoders provide relative position information by generating pulses when the shaft rotates. They are cost-effective and commonly used in applications such as robotics and industrial automation. Absolute shaft encoders, on the other hand, provide absolute position information, allowing the exact position to be determined even after power loss. They are widely used in applications where precise position control is required, such as in CNC machines and medical equipment.</p></p>
