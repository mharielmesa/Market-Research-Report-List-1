<p><ul><li>ARF Racing Drone</li><li>RTF Racing Drone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Racing Drone market is divided into two types: ARF (Almost Ready to Fly) Racing Drones, and RTF (Ready to Fly) Racing Drones. ARF Racing Drones come partially assembled, requiring additional components such as a flight controller, receiver, and battery to be installed by the user. This gives the user flexibility in customizing their drone to their preferences. On the other hand, RTF Racing Drones come fully assembled and ready to fly out of the box, making them suitable for beginners or users who prefer a hassle-free experience. Both types cater to different skill levels and customization needs within the racing drone market.</p></p>
