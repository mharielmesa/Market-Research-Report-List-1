<p><strong>The Fertilizing Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spreaders</li><li>Sprayers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fertilizing machinery market consists of two main types: spreaders and sprayers. Spreaders are machines used to evenly distribute fertilizers onto the soil. These machines come in various forms, such as broadcast spreaders and drop spreaders, and can be operated manually or mounted onto vehicles. Sprayers, on the other hand, are used to apply liquid chemically-based or organic fertilizers onto crops. They ensure an even application of fertilizers and can be operated manually or attached to tractors or other equipment. Both spreaders and sprayers play a crucial role in efficient and effective fertilization practices in agriculture.</p></p>
