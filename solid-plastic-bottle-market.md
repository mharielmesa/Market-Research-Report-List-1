<p><p>The solid plastic bottle market is categorized into various types based on the material used. PP bottles are known for their heat resistance and are commonly used for storing chemicals and pharmaceuticals. HDPE bottles are durable and have a high resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for packaging cleaning products and oils. LDPE bottles are flexible and often used for packaging squeezable products like lotions and shampoos.
