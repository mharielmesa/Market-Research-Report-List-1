<p><strong>The Changeover Manifold Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Changeover Manifold</li><li>Semi-automatic Changeover Manifold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Changeover manifold market is divided into two types: Automatic Changeover Manifold and Semi-automatic Changeover Manifold. </p><p>The Automatic Changeover Manifold is designed to automatically switch between cylinders without any manual intervention. It uses advanced sensors and controls to detect when one cylinder is empty and seamlessly switches to another full cylinder, ensuring a continuous supply of gas or liquid without interruption.</p><p>On the other hand, the Semi-automatic Changeover Manifold requires manual intervention to switch between cylinders. It typically features manual valves that need to be manually adjusted to switch from one cylinder to another, making it less automated compared to the automatic version.</p><p>Both types of changeover manifolds are essential for applications that require a consistent and uninterrupted supply of gas or liquid, but the automatic version offers a higher level of convenience and efficiency by eliminating the need for manual intervention.</p></p>
