<p><p>The Solar Tracking System market consists of two main types: Single Axis Solar Tracking System and Dual Axis Solar Tracking System. Single Axis Solar Tracking Systems track the movement of the sun along a single axis, either horizontally or vertically. This allows the solar panels to maximize their exposure to sunlight throughout the day. On the other hand, Dual Axis Solar Tracking Systems track the movement of the sun along both horizontal and vertical axes. This enables the solar panels to constantly adjust their position to maintain optimal sunlight absorption throughout the day.</p></p>
