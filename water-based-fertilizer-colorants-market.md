<p><p>Water-based fertilizer colorants are used to enhance the appearance of liquid fertilizers by adding color. The market for these colorants can be divided into two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are colorants that are soluble in water and provide a translucent color, while pigments are insoluble in water and provide a more opaque and vibrant color. Both types of colorants are used in the agriculture industry to help identify and differentiate between different types of fertilizers and to improve their visual appeal.</p></p>
