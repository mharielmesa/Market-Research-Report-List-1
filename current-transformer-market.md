<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The current transformer (CT) market comprises various types of transformers based on their construction and insulation medium. Dry type CTs use air or solid insulation, making them suitable for indoor applications. Pouring type CTs are filled with epoxy resin for insulation and are used in outdoor installations. Oil-immersed CTs are immersed in transformer oil for better insulation and cooling. Gas-insulated CTs use SF6 gas as insulation and are commonly used in high-voltage applications. Each type has its advantages in terms of reliability, safety, and performance and is selected based on specific requirements.</p></p>
